Support Our Mission

Why Support Us?

Your donations help us continue our mission of advancing aerospace education and innovation. With your support, we can:

  • Purchase materials and equipment for rocket construction
  • Fund travel to regional and national competitions
  • Provide educational workshops and training for members
  • Expand our outreach programs to inspire the next generation

Sponsorship Tiers

Bronze Tier - $500

  • Logo on club website
  • Acknowledgment in presentations
  • Certificate of appreciation

Silver Tier - $1,000

  • All Bronze benefits
  • Logo on team t-shirts
  • Social media recognition
  • Invitation to launch events

Gold Tier - $2,500

  • All Silver benefits
  • Logo on rocket body
  • Company feature in newsletter
  • Presentation to your organization

Platinum Tier - $5,000+

  • All Gold benefits
  • Primary logo placement on rocket
  • Named sponsorship of a mission
  • Exclusive demo day for your organization
  • Priority access to student recruitment
