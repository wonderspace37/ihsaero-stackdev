Past Missions

Horizon I

Launch Date: June 15, 2023

Maximum Altitude: 10,200 ft

Our most recent high-altitude mission featuring a custom-designed dual deployment recovery system and atmospheric data collection payload.

Key achievements:

  • Successful deployment of main and drogue parachutes
  • Complete data collection throughout flight
  • Recovery with minimal damage

Voyager II

Launch Date: November 5, 2022

Maximum Altitude: 8,750 ft

A mid-power rocket designed for the NASA Student Launch competition, featuring a payload designed to collect soil samples.

Key achievements:

  • Successful deployment of innovative soil collection mechanism
  • Precise landing within 250 feet of target area
  • Complete telemetry data transmission throughout flight

Pioneer I

Launch Date: March 20, 2022

Maximum Altitude: 5,280 ft

Our first major project as a team, featuring a commercially available motor and custom airframe.

Key achievements:

  • First successful launch and recovery
  • Validation of our design and construction methods
  • Established baseline for future projects
