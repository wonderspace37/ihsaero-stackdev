Prospectus

Membership Requirements

Joining our rocketry club is open to all enthusiasts, regardless of experience level. Here's what you need to know:

  • Open to all students and community members
  • No prior experience required - we provide training
  • Commitment to attend weekly meetings
  • Participation in at least one technical team
  • Annual membership fee: $50 (covers basic materials and club activities)

Learning Opportunities

As a member of our club, you'll have access to various learning opportunities:

  • Hands-on experience with rocket design and construction
  • Training workshops on CAD, simulation software, and electronics
  • Mentorship from experienced members and faculty advisors
  • Networking with aerospace industry professionals
  • Participation in regional and national competitions

Corporate Sponsorship

We rely on corporate sponsors to help fund our projects and competitions. Sponsoring our club offers several benefits:

  • Brand visibility on our rockets, website, and promotional materials
  • Access to talented students for potential recruitment
  • Demonstration of corporate commitment to STEM education
  • Tax-deductible contributions
  • Recognition at all club events and competitions

For more information on sponsorship opportunities, please visit our Donations page.

Get Involved

Ready to join our journey to the stars? Contact us at info@rocketryclub.org or attend one of our weekly meetings every Thursday at 6:00 PM in the Engineering Building, Room 305.
